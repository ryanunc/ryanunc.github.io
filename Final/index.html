      Chengdu City

      Located in the fertile Sichuan Basin, Chengdu is surrounded by mountains and rivers, offering a perfect balance between urban development and natural beauty.

      Discover More

      About Chengdu

      Adventure

      Chengdu is the perfect destination for adventure seekers. Explore the scenic trails of Qingcheng Mountain or enjoy rafting on the Minjiang River. Whether you love hiking, climbing, or just immersing yourself in nature, Chengdu offers endless opportunities.

      Cuisine

      Chengdu is the heart of Sichuan cuisine. From the spicy kick of hot pot to the comforting flavors of Dan Dan Noodles, every meal here is a feast for your taste buds. Don’t miss the bustling food markets and traditional tea houses.

      History

      With over 2,300 years of history, Chengdu is a treasure trove of cultural heritage. Visit the Wuhou Shrine, the ancient Dujiangyan Irrigation System, or the Jinsha Site Museum to discover the city’s fascinating past.

      Festivals

      Chengdu’s festivals are a vibrant celebration of its culture. From the glowing Lantern Festival to the lively Dragon Boat Festival, each event is a chance to experience the city’s traditions and enjoy its joyful atmosphere.

      Relaxation

      Discover Chengdu’s tranquil side with serene teahouses and traditional hot springs. Unwind at People’s Park while sipping tea brewed the Sichuan way or enjoy a soothing spa day surrounded by lush bamboo forests.

      Shopping

      Chengdu offers a mix of traditional and modern shopping experiences. Wander through lively street markets for unique handmade crafts or visit luxury malls like IFS to find high-end fashion. Shopping here is as diverse as the city itself.
