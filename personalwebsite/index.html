To Shoot or Not to Shoot

A photography enthusiast, whose main work involves capturing others, finds herself increasingly haunted by a question: Shoot or Not to Shoot?

As models take the stage, photographers freely command them to strike poses, commands the models instinctively obey. This subconscious compliance prompts a deep ethical dilemma: Is it truly ethical? Could it inadvertently infringe upon models' human rights? Contemplating these questions leads her to cease shooting, plunging her into a dilemma.
